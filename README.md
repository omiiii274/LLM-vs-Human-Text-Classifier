> Uses linguistic fingerprinting, explainable AI, and multiple feature engineering strategies.

---

## 🎯 Why This Matters

As LLMs become embedded in everyday workflows, the ability to detect AI-generated content is critical for:

- 🎓 **Academic integrity** — detecting AI-written essays and submissions
- 🛒 **E-commerce trust** — identifying fake AI-generated product reviews
- 📰 **Social media authenticity** — spotting AI-generated misinformation
- 📋 **Clinical documentation** — verifying human-authored medical notes

---

## ✨ Key Technical Highlights

### 🔹 Linguistic Analysis
- **N-Gram analysis** to capture structural patterns in writing
- **Flesch Reading Ease Score** — AI text tends to score differently to human writing
- **Lexical Diversity** — humans use more varied vocabulary than LLMs in many contexts

### 🔹 Feature Engineering (4 Strategies)
| Method | What It Captures |
|---|---|
| TF-IDF | Word frequency weighted by document rarity |
| Bag of Words | Raw term frequency |
| Sentence Embeddings | Semantic meaning of full sentences |
| LLM-based Embeddings | Deep contextual representations |

### 🔹 Explainable AI (XAI)
- **SHAP** — global feature importance showing which linguistic features most distinguish AI from human text
- **LIME** — local explanations showing why a specific text was classified as AI or human
- Full transparency into model decision-making — critical for real-world deployment

### 🔹 Visual EDA
- **Word Clouds** comparing AI vs human vocabulary
- **Frequency analysis** of distinctive patterns
- **Complexity score distributions** across both classes

## 🔍 How Linguistic Fingerprints Work

Humans and LLMs leave different traces in text:

| Property | Human Writing | LLM Writing |
|---|---|---|
| Lexical diversity | Higher variance | More consistent |
| Sentence length | More varied | More uniform |
| Flesch score | Wider range | Tends to cluster |
| Punctuation patterns | Irregular | More predictable |
| N-gram repetition | Lower | Higher (formulaic phrases) |

SHAP values reveal exactly which of these features drives each prediction.
